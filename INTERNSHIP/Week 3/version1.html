Why is sales enablement crucial?

The average salesperson spends about 440 hours each year trying to find the right content to share with their prospects and customers.

Companies that use sales enablement platforms see a 71% improvement in sales.

Sales reps spend 40% of their time in creating relevant content for customers and prospects.

Transform sales capability of frontline salesforce with SmartSell

SmartSell is a frictionless mobile platform that helps your salespeople across the sales cycle by delivering accurate, up-to-date, targeted content at the right stage of the sales cycle. It allows them to quickly and easily understand, show, or share collateral with prospects and customers. The collateral can be made relevant and personalized to the customer’s specific requirements on the mobile app.

How SmartSell drives sales productivity

Crunches sales cycle

Relevant content at every stage of your sales cycle increases conversion rates, and reduces the number of calls/meetings required to close deals.

Eliminates friction

Creating and sharing customized collateral on mobile makes it very easy for salespeople to have effective conversations in customer meetings.

Facilitates real-time content updates

Push product updates, competitor information, policy changes, industry news, and company bulletins in ready-to-use formats in an instant.

Tracks effectiveness, and drive adoption

Configurable dashboard and tracking makes it easy to monitor usage across the sales cycle. Surveys, quizzes, and notifications drive adoption.
